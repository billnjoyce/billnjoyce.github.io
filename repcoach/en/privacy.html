PART A

Collection & Use

1.Overview

Joyce Studios (the “Company”) operates the mobile app RepCoach (the “Service”) and protects users' personal data in accordance with applicable law. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, why, how we protect it, and your rights.

At a glance · Workouts you enter are stored on your device by default. · They are backed up to the cloud only if you enable sign-in/sync. · We do not sell your data. · Free users may see Google AdMob ads. · We do not connect to medical/healthcare devices.

We do not collect precise location, contacts, address book, or financial-account access.

3.How We Collect

We collect data (a) when you enter/create it in the app, (b) via Apple at sign-in, (c) via Apple StoreKit on subscription, (d) from anonymous statistics and device info generated during use, and (e) via Google AdMob when ads are shown.

4.How We Use Data

  • Provide the Service: store workouts; deliver AI coaching, recommendations, analytics
  • Backup & sync: back up and sync User Content across signed-in devices
  • Subscription: verify and maintain Pro entitlement
  • Notifications: send workout reminders and weekly coaching alerts
  • Advertising: show ads to free users (removed with Pro)
  • Improvement: aggregate anonymous stats to improve quality, diagnose errors, prevent abuse

5.Retention & Deletion

User Content is stored on your device and, if sync is enabled, in the cloud while your account is active. You can delete it anytime via “Delete all data” or account deletion, which also removes the corresponding cloud data. Deleting the app removes local data. Payment records may be retained by Apple and as required by law.

PART B

Sharing · International Transfer

6.Sharing & Processors

We do not share personal data with third parties except with your consent or as required by law, and we do not sell data. We use the following processors to operate the Service.

7.Payment Data (Apple)

Subscription payments are processed by Apple via the App Store. We do not collect or store card/payment-method information. For entitlement checks and anonymous revenue statistics, we process only non-identifying payment metadata (transaction ID, product, price, currency, storefront).

8.International Transfer

For backup, sync, authentication, and advertising, User Content and account identifiers may be stored/processed on servers operated by Apple and Google (Firebase/AdMob) located abroad (e.g., the United States). By using the Service you consent to such transfers. We apply reasonable safeguards including encryption in transit.

PART C

Rights · Security · Contact

9.Your Rights

You may request access, correction, deletion, or suspension of processing of your personal data at any time. Most data can be viewed, edited, and deleted directly in the app (including “Delete all data”); for further requests, contact us below. We honor rights under applicable law, including GDPR and CCPA where applicable.

10.Security

  • Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Authentication-based access control — only authenticated users access their own data
  • Pseudonymization, least-privilege and data-minimization principles, access logging

11.Cookies · Tracking · Advertising

Free users may see ads via Google AdMob, which may use advertising identifiers (e.g., IDFA). Under Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT), you may choose whether to allow tracking and can limit ad tracking in device settings. Subscribing to RepCoach Pro removes ads. We do not track your activity for purposes other than advertising.

12.Children

The Service is not directed to children under the age required in your jurisdiction (e.g., under 14 in Korea). If we learn that a child's data was collected without guardian consent, we delete it without delay.

13.Apple App Privacy Label

The App Store “App Privacy” section summarizes our data practices alongside this policy. Where they differ, this policy provides the more detailed explanation.
