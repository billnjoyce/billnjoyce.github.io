PART 1

Collection & Use

1.Overview

joycestudios (the "Company") operates the mobile app Tradeflect (the "Service") and respects your privacy in accordance with applicable laws. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, why, and how we protect it.

At a glance · Data you enter (trade records) is stored on your device by default. · It is backed up to the cloud only if you enable sign-in/sync. · We do not sell your data and use no advertising-tracking SDKs. · We do not connect to brokers or banks.

We do not collect precise location, contacts, address book, or access to financial accounts.

3.How We Collect

We collect information (a) when you enter it in the app, (b) via Apple/Google when you sign in, (c) via Apple StoreKit when you subscribe, and (d) as anonymous usage statistics generated during use.

4.How We Use Information

  • Provide the Service: store trades; deliver statistics, analysis, and reviews.
  • Backup & sync: back up and synchronize Your Content across signed-in devices.
  • Subscription management: verify and maintain Pro entitlement.
  • Improvement: improve quality and diagnose errors using anonymous, aggregated statistics.

5.Retention & Deletion

Your Content is stored on your device and, if sync is enabled, in the cloud while your account remains active. You can delete your data anytime using "Erase all data" in the app, which also deletes the corresponding cloud data. Payment-related records may be retained by Apple and as required by law.

Sharing · Transfers

We do not share personal data with third parties except with your consent or as required by law. We use the following processors to operate the Service:

7.Payment Data (Apple)

Subscription payments are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store your card or payment-method details. We process only non-identifying payment metadata (transaction identifier, product, price, currency, storefront) to verify entitlement and compile anonymous revenue statistics.

8.International Transfers

For backup, sync, and authentication, Your Content and account identifiers may be stored and processed on servers operated by Google (Firebase) outside your country. By using the Service you consent to such transfer. We apply reasonable safeguards, including encryption in transit.

PART 3

Rights · Protection · Contact

9.Your Rights

You may access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal data at any time. Most data can be viewed, edited, and deleted directly in the app (including "Erase all data"); for additional requests, contact us below. We honor rights under applicable laws (including GDPR and CCPA where they apply).

10.Security

  • Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS).
  • Authentication-based access control and least-privilege principles.
  • Pseudonymization and access logging.

11.Cookies, Tracking & Ads

The Service does not use advertising-identifier (IDFA) tracking or third-party ad SDKs, and does not track your activity for advertising purposes.

12.Children

The Service is not directed to children under the age required by your jurisdiction (e.g., under 14 in Korea). If we learn that such data was collected without the required guardian consent, we will delete it promptly.

13.Apple App Privacy Label

The "App Privacy" section on the App Store summarizes our data practices alongside this Policy. Where they differ, this Policy provides the more detailed explanation.
