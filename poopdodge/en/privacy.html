Privacy Policy

App Name: Poop Dodge: Endless Survival
Package Name: com.joycestudios.poopdodge
Developer: Joyce Studios
Last Updated: May 20, 2026

Joyce Studios ("the Company", "we", "us", or "our") respects the privacy of users of "Poop Dodge: Endless Survival" ("the App") and complies with applicable data-protection laws, including the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), the Personal Information Protection Act of Korea, and the Google Play User Data policy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect through the App, how we use, store, and share it, and what rights you have regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide the App. The App does not require sign-up and does not collect directly identifying information such as your name, phone number, or email address.

1-1. Information Collected Automatically

Data How It Is Collected Purpose
Hashed device identifier (a hash of ANDROID_ID) Generated automatically on first launch To identify and manage your score records on a per-device basis
FCM Token (Firebase Cloud Messaging Token) Issued automatically when you grant push-notification permission To send push notifications when the global high score is updated
Advertising ID (AAID) Collected by the Google AdMob SDK To serve personalized ads and measure ad performance (you may limit this in device settings)
App usage logs (scores, play time, crash logs) Collected automatically while you use the App Leaderboard operation, anti-cheat detection, and stability improvement
Approximate location (country/region level, based on IP) Collected automatically when you connect to our servers Regional ad delivery and aggregate service statistics

1-2. Information You Provide Directly

Data How It Is Collected Purpose
Nickname You enter it inside the App Displayed on the global leaderboard
Selected character You choose it inside the App Gameplay and leaderboard display

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information only for the following purposes. If we ever need to use it for a new purpose, we will obtain your consent first.

  • To provide the core game service (score recording, character selection, nickname display)
  • To operate the global leaderboard (top 1,000 players worldwide)
  • To send push notifications when the global high score is updated
  • To detect and prevent cheating (score manipulation, automation tools)
  • To deliver ads via Google AdMob (banner and interstitial ads)
  • To improve app stability through crash and performance analysis
  • To respond to user inquiries

3. Data Retention

  • Hashed device identifier, nickname, score, character data: Retained until you uninstall the App or request deletion.
  • FCM token: Retained until you disable push notifications or uninstall the App.
  • App usage and crash logs: Automatically deleted after a maximum of 90 days.
  • Records of detected cheating: Retained for up to 1 year to prevent recurrence, then deleted.

Data is deleted without delay once the retention period expires or the processing purpose is fulfilled.

4. Data Sharing and Third-Party Processors

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your data with third parties except for the service providers listed below, who process data on our behalf to operate the App.

Provider Purpose Data Processed Privacy Policy
Google LLC (Firebase) Server hosting, push notifications, database, analytics Hashed device identifier, FCM token, nickname, score firebase.google.com/support/privacy
Google LLC (AdMob) Ad delivery and measurement Advertising ID (AAID), approximate location, device info policies.google.com/privacy

5. Data Security

We implement the following measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption in transit: All communication between the App and our servers is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS.
  • Identifier hashing: We store a one-way hash rather than the raw ANDROID_ID, preventing reverse lookup.
  • Access control: Only authorized operators can access user data.
  • Data minimization: We collect only what is necessary to provide the service.

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data, regardless of where you live:

  • Right to access: Request a copy of your nickname, score, and leaderboard records.
  • Right to rectify: Change your nickname directly within the App at any time.
  • Right to delete: Request deletion of your score and leaderboard records via the contact email below.
  • Right to restrict processing: Disable push notifications in your device settings.
  • Right to opt out of ad tracking: Reset your Advertising ID or opt out of personalized ads in Device Settings > Google > Ads.
  • Right to data portability (GDPR): Request a copy of your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Right to withdraw consent: Withdraw your consent at any time without affecting prior lawful processing.

We will respond to deletion requests and delete the relevant data within 30 days of receiving your request.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is a casual game suitable for general audiences, but we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 (per the US Children's Online Privacy Protection Act, COPPA) or under the age of 14 (per Korean law). If a parent or guardian becomes aware that their child's information has been collected, please contact us at the email below and we will delete it promptly.

8. Advertising

  • The App displays banner and interstitial ads through Google AdMob.
  • AdMob may use the Advertising ID (AAID) to serve personalized ads.
  • You can reset your Advertising ID or opt out of personalized ads in your Android device's [Settings > Google > Ads] menu.
  • Users in the European Economic Area (EEA) will be shown a GDPR-compliant consent form for ad personalization.
  • Users in California have additional rights under the CCPA, including the right to opt out of any "sale" of personal information. We do not sell personal information.

9. International Data Transfers

Because we use Google Firebase and AdMob, some of your data may be processed in Google's global data centers, including in the United States.

  • Recipient: Google LLC
  • Destination country: United States (Google global data centers)
  • Data transferred: Hashed device identifier, FCM token, nickname, score, Advertising ID
  • Purpose: Server hosting, push notifications, ad delivery
  • Safeguards: Standard Contractual Clauses (SCC) approved by the European Commission, where applicable.
  • Retention period: As described in Section 3 of this Policy.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in law or our service. Material changes will be announced via in-app notice or push notification before they take effect. The updated policy becomes effective on the date it is posted on this page.
